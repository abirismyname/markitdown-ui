🔐 Privacy Policy

Short version: we collect nothing. Your files stay on your Mac.

MarkyMarkdown is a local-only tool. It makes no network connections and stores no data beyond writing the converted .md file to your disk.

📋 What we collect

Nothing. MarkyMarkdown collects no personal data, no usage statistics, no crash reports, and no analytics of any kind.

⚙️ How your files are processed

When you drag a file onto MarkyMarkdown, the following happens — entirely on your Mac:

  • The file path is passed to the bundled MarkItDown command-line binary.
  • MarkItDown reads the file from disk and writes a .md output file to the same directory.
  • The output is displayed in the app. No content is read, stored, or transmitted by MarkyMarkdown.

Your files are never uploaded, never copied to a server, and never seen by anyone other than you.

🌐 Network access

MarkyMarkdown makes zero network requests at any time — not on launch, not during conversion, not in the background. You can verify this with a network monitor such as Little Snitch or Wireshark.

📊 Analytics & tracking

There are no analytics, no tracking pixels, no crash-reporting SDKs, and no telemetry of any kind in MarkyMarkdown. No third-party code runs inside the app.

🔑 Accounts

MarkyMarkdown does not require an account and does not support creating one. There is no sign-in, no subscription, and no registration of any kind.

👶 Children

Because no personal data is collected at all, MarkyMarkdown does not require any special handling under children's privacy regulations (COPPA, GDPR-K, etc.).
